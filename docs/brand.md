# Brand

## Brand Position

Eliya Kapach is a filmmaker, director, and visual storyteller specializing in cinematic films for brands, hotels, tourism, outdoor companies, and meaningful stories.

The brand represents craftsmanship over trends. Every project is driven by emotion, atmosphere, and storytelling.

## Mission

Create films that make people feel something.

## Core Values

- Authenticity
- Craftsmanship
- Emotion
- Adventure
- Curiosity
- Attention to Detail
- Human Connection
- Minimalism

## Brand Personality

Confident, calm, professional, curious, creative, modern.

Never arrogant. Never flashy.

## Brand References

Think: A24, Apple, Patagonia, Arc'teryx, Land Rover, The North Face, DJI, Visit Iceland, National Geographic.

## Keywords

cinematic, premium, immersive, storytelling, emotion, travel, adventure, nature, luxury, human, craft
