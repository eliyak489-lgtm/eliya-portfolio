Who I am

I'm Eliya — filmmaker, content creator, and visual storyteller.

I work with brands that want more than good-looking content —

My work is rooted in real experiences: travel, speed, silence, movement. I translate those into stories that carry emotion, depth, and presence. Every project is led by me, end-to-end, with full creative control.

The result?
Films that don't just look impressive — they stay with people.
